Crispy Smashed Potatoes

Description

Smashed potatoes are soft on the inside and crunchy on the outside. They're incredibly easy to season with a wide range of flavors available. They pair well with any dish!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces baby yellow potatoes, washed
  • Water
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (and more for drizzling)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add potatoes to large pot and fill with water until they are submerged. Add 1 tsp salt and bring to a boil
  2. Once boiling, cook for 5 minutes or until tender with a fork
  3. Preheat oven to 220 degrees C. Line large baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone mat
  4. Pour 1/4 cup olive oil onto baking sheet and spread evenly. Add cooked potatoes to sheet
  5. Lightly oil bottom of mug or glass, and press down on each potato until it's about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick. Leave space between each potato
  6. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 tsp of salt, garlic powder, black pepper, and optional seasonings. Drizzle more olive oil on top if desired
  7. Roast for 30 minutes, checking after 15 minutes. Cook until edges are golden and crispy
  8. Remove from oven and season with more salt and pepper if desired.
